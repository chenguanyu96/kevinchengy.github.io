CourseConnect
Project Web Application Agile
CourseConnect is web application that is meant to serve as a replacement to the current Blackboard (Learning Management System) that UofT uses. This webapp allows students, professors and TAs to communicate via a forum and a chat. It also gives students the opportunity to connect with each other, this can help students form teams for group projects and help with learning.
